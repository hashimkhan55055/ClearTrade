WEBOC Guidance

Step-by-Step WEBOC Import Process

ClearTrade takes you through each WEBOC step so you never get lost in Pakistan's customs system.

1

Register on WEBOC

Register your NTN/STRN on weboc.gov.pk. ClearTrade guides you through the registration documents required.

2

File Goods Declaration (GD)

ClearTrade auto-fills your GD with the correct HS code, duty rates, and all required fields.

3

Pay Duties & Taxes

Receive your Payment Slip Number (PSN). Pay via bank. ClearTrade shows exact amounts before you pay.

4

Customs Examination

Your consignment is assigned a channel. ClearTrade tells you what documents to keep ready for each channel.

5

Release & Delivery

Once cleared, collect your Bill of Entry. ClearTrade tracks status and alerts you at each stage.
